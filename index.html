About us

Success tutorial is an educational institute originated for training of students preparing for competitive entrance examinations like IIT-JEE (JEE Advanced & JEE Main) for admissions to premier engineering institutions like Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), National Institutes of Technology (NITs) and NEET for AIMS and other ellite institute.
We also engaged in training, guiding and orienting high school students for their studies, choosing their career and living a thoughtful and balanced life.

Courses

JEE, NEET, CET

Goal of the program is to help student to get a Top rank in JEE(Main & Advanced), NEET & CET. Students joining this program have more time to clear their fundamentals and practice as per corresponding exam.

11th & 12th

Course prepare students for theire higher secondary examination at comfortable pace and detailed manner.

Std 5th to 10th

Course focus on a conceptual base which helps the students emerge successful in school examinations. This course will build a strong foundation for highschool.

Regular classes

Regular lecture classes 5 days a week. Each 45 minute lecture include theoretical & conceptual learning

Doubt sessions

1 on 1 engaging doubt sessions are conducted after each lessions

Periodic test and test series

We conduct weekly test on saturday and solve student's doubts to remove their fear of exam and to make them confident during their finals.

Technology for learning

We use technology to enhance teaching & learning experience. On our app, student can access our exclusive notes, e-library and video library to build strong fundamentals and for bettter understanding of concept

Excellant faculty

Outstanding and highly qualified faculty members who help students in every aspect of their study life. Faculty focus on individual student & help them as per performance report to eliminate their weekness in any particular subject or concepts.

Performance oriented

Students can check their exam performance on our institute app from day 1 to end of course, where they will know their strong & weak subjects, lessons, concepts and get them strong.

App on Google Playstore

An app for students & their parents to track students performance, our exclusive class notes, paperset & solution, e-library, video library, attendance, timetable, class announcements.

Director

 

A.Z.Kapoor

Mr. Arnav Kapoor, an edupreneur with success in cracking IIT-JEE, was born in a small village in the Kota district in the state of Rajasthan. He excelled throughout his schooling, in himself clearing the IIT-JEE and as an Electrical & Electronics Engineering student at IIT-Madras. After completing his B.Tech. in 1994, he joined BHEL as a Trainee Engineer .Following a brief stint at BHEL, destiny brought him into coaching education which at that time was emerging in Kota and he joined a reputed coaching institute in Kota as part of the Physics Faculty in 1995 and continued there till 2001.
Through his sincerity, dedication and hard work, he earned respect from his students and became very popular amongst the student fraternity, as AZK Sir , for his in-depth subject knowledge, unmatched teaching skills and passionate aptitude for the coaching profession. The dream of becoming an edupreneur resulted in the genesis of Success, founded by Mr . A.Z. Kapoor in 2001 at Kota itself. Since then, institute has taken leaps and bounds forward under his leadership and his persistent quest to take it to the pinnacle of success & excellence.
